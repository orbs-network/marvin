<h1 align="center"> Dredd </h1>
<p align="center">
  <b >Dredd will judge your tests and decide whether they passed or failed.</b>
</p>

<br>

Dredd receives a job ID and asks [Marvin's](https://github.com/orbs-network/marvin) for the job's results.
